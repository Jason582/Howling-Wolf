A brand refresh of the iconic Western Australian Howling Wolves began with a love of the wine. These premium wines are nestled in the lush-green, rolling landscape of the Margaret River Wine Region, just below the beautiful Yallingup, Metricup, and Cowaramup vineyard region. It's one of the best wineries in Margaret River.

Why I decided to rebrand

Well to start with I'm a big fan of the wine, but I think in its current state the brand isn't working. A quick look at the current Howling Wolf website will show, at least from a user's point of view, the design is basic and there are quite a few errors.

A step in the right direction

So I thought I would play on the name a bit with the design and did a bit of research into grapes...
'Resveratrol is a stilbenoid, a type of natural phenol, and a phytoalexin produced by several plants in response to injury or when the plant is under attack by pathogens, such as bacteria or fungi. Sources of resveratrol in food include the skin of grapes'...

...and maybe a mobile app?

It may be worth considering at this stage pondering the thought of a prototype, at least in its basic format. After all, buying wine is a pastime of many a customer.
